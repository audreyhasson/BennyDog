Benny Hasson is a Labrador Retriever from Ellicott City. He was born on April 24th, 2011. He is loved dearly by his three sisters and his Mom and Dad. Currently, Benny is employed as the Office Dog of About Faces. In the past, he has won the Goodest Boy and Soundest Sleeper awards. He also holds the world record for Missing the Most Snacks Thrown in the Air in a Row. Benny enjoys long walks on the beach, chicken sticks, and making friends.
