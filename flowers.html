I'm not sure how I'm supposed to feel about flowers. I tried eating them once, but they tasted like sneezes. I don't like sneezes. When I sneeze, the floor normally hits my snout. This confuses me a lot. I never see it happen, even when I try really hard to keep my eyes open. On the other hand, flowers are kind of floofy, like really small pillows. Pillows mean naps, and I like naps a lot. Now that I think about it, this is actually a really confusing topic. I wasn’t ready to feel this conflicted. Can we talk about something else?
