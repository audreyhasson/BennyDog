Is Vogue looking for models? Because they can stop looking right now. Honestly, I impress myself. And the beach is like my favorite place ever, even when I have to wake up super early to see the sunrise. I’m not sure what the big deal is. From what I can tell, the same thing happens every day. But I do like sand a lot. I also like running in circles around the beach. Really big circles. Sometimes I go into the water, and I can hear Dad cheering me on, “No, Benny! Don’t get all wet!” My favorite is when I make friends with other people, normally through my tried-and-true jump and lick strategy. I’m really good at making friends.
